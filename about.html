About Me

Hello! You can call me Pavani. I am a BTech 4th year student passionate about technology and innovation. I love learning new technologies, building projects, and exploring the endless possibilities in the world of computer science and engineering.

I was born in 2003 in Hyderabad, India. I am currently pursuing my BTech in Computer Science at a university in Hyderabad. Out of my passion for technology, I started to learn web development and have been diving deep into full-stack development ever since.

As part of my learning journey, I started contributing to open-source projects and building my own applications. I hope to use my skills to create impactful solutions in the tech world. Hopefully, it can help you too.
